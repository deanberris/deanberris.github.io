Insights from Chilling Out

I am a logorrheic geek, writing about my adventures and experiences.

I am a migrant to Sydney, New South Wales, Australia from Manila, Philippines who has been around the IT industry for long enough to have enough stories to share. I've been a student, an entrepreneur, an employee, and a consultant. I'm also currently a husband to an amazing wife, a father to two amazing kids, and a software engineer in a big tech company.

This is my latest experiment in building and establishing relationships with like-minded people out to make the world a better place through technology. This space is dedicated to highlighting not only who I am but a collection of thoughts going out through my newsletter.
